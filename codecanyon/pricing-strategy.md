## License Types Explained

### Regular License ($29-49)
- Use in ONE end product
- End product is FREE for end users
- Example: Your own content generation site

### Extended License ($149-249)
- Use in ONE end product
- End product can be SOLD to end users
- Example: Selling as SaaS to customers

## Promotional Strategies

### Launch Week
1. Set price at $29 (launch discount)
2. Announce on social media
3. Post in relevant forums/communities
4. Create YouTube demo video

### First Month
1. Respond to ALL comments within 24 hours
2. Fix any bugs immediately
3. Add small features based on feedback
4. Ask happy customers for reviews

### Ongoing
1. Release updates monthly
2. Add new templates quarterly
3. Consider bundle deals
4. Cross-promote with other authors

## Discount Strategies

### When to Offer Discounts
- Envato sales events (Black Friday, etc.)
- Slow sales periods
- Major updates
- Bundle with other products

### Discount Amounts
- 10-20%: Minor promotions
- 30-40%: Major sales events
- 50%: Clearance (not recommended for new items)
